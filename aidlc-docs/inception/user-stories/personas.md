# Personas — Smwdle

## P1 — Casual Daily Player ("Mina")
- **Profile**: Plays daily browser puzzles (Wordle, onepiecedle) during breaks. Knows Summoners War casually.
- **Goals**: A quick, fun 2–5 minute daily challenge; keep a streak; share results with friends.
- **Motivations**: Habit, light competition, bragging rights.
- **Frustrations**: Needing to sign up; confusing hints; losing progress; slow pages.
- **Devices**: Primarily mobile.
- **Key needs**: Instant play (no login), clear hints, easy share, persistent streak.

## P2 — Summoners War Veteran ("Krit")
- **Profile**: Long-time SW player who knows monster families, elements, and natural-star rarity well.
- **Goals**: Solve in as few guesses as possible; be tested on deep roster knowledge; compare guess counts with the community.
- **Motivations**: Mastery, showing off game knowledge.
- **Frustrations**: Incorrect/outdated monster data; answers pulled from ultra-obscure monsters that feel unfair; ambiguous attribute definitions.
- **Devices**: Desktop and mobile.
- **Key needs**: Accurate data, fair curated answer pool, precise attribute comparisons, full-roster guessing.

## P3 — Accessibility-Focused Player ("Sam")
- **Profile**: Player with color-vision deficiency who enjoys puzzle games.
- **Goals**: Play and understand hints without relying on color alone.
- **Motivations**: Same fun as everyone else, unobstructed by design choices.
- **Frustrations**: Green/grey hints indistinguishable; small tap targets; no keyboard navigation.
- **Devices**: Desktop with keyboard; mobile.
- **Key needs**: Icon/text + color redundancy, readable contrast, keyboard-navigable autocomplete, screen-reader-friendly results.
